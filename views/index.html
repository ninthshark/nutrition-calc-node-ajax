A quick & easy way to calculate nutritional information and labels for your recipes

Information taken from UK Food Standards Agency (FSA) Composition of foods integrated dataset (CoF IDS)

Ever wondered, how do they calculate nutrition value on the back of food packages we buy from supermarkets.

Here is the app for you to create your own recipes and calculate nutrition information on the tip of your fingers.

Follow these easy steps

  1. Search ingredients from food database.
  2. Add quantity per recipe.
  3. Add ingredient to the recipe section.
  4. Click the button to analyse your recipe.

Note:Ingredients measurements are in grams for accuracy
